Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Vault Channels ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you interact with our content on third-party platforms including TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information from viewers. When you view, like, comment on, or share our content, the respective platform (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram) may collect information about your interactions in accordance with their own privacy policies. We may receive aggregated, anonymized analytics data from these platforms such as:

  • View counts and watch time
  • Geographic distribution of viewers
  • Engagement metrics (likes, shares, comments)
  • Device and browser type (aggregated only)

2. How We Use Information

The analytics data we receive is used solely to:

  • Understand what content our audience finds valuable
  • Improve the quality and relevance of our videos
  • Determine optimal posting times and frequencies

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any personally identifiable information to outside parties.

3. Third-Party Platforms

Our content is published on the following platforms, each with their own privacy policies:

  • TikTok: TikTok Privacy Policy
  • YouTube: Google Privacy Policy
  • Instagram: Instagram Privacy Policy

We encourage you to review these policies as they govern the collection and use of your data on those platforms.

4. Cookies

We do not operate a website that sets cookies directly. Any cookies associated with our content are set by the third-party platforms (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram) in accordance with their own cookie policies.

5. Children's Privacy

Our content is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove that information.

6. Data Retention

We retain aggregated analytics data for as long as necessary to improve our content. We do not retain personally identifiable information.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data including:

  • The right to access information held about you
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to erasure of your data
  • The right to object to processing

To exercise these rights with respect to data held by the platforms, please contact TikTok, YouTube, or Instagram directly through their respective privacy controls.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: vaultchannels@gmail.com
